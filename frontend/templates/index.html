Our Mission

USETAI is a research-first startup building scalable, ethical AI systems that assist humans in complex decision-making and everyday life. We combine systems thinking, robust ML, and practical engineering to deliver solutions that matter.

Our vision is to create AI that empowers individuals and organizations to reach their full potential while maintaining human agency and control.

Sample Initiatives

Climate-Assist

Decision-support systems for climate adaptation planning. We help organizations understand and prepare for climate-related challenges through AI-powered analytics and scenario modeling.

Health Navigator

AI triage and workflow optimization for clinics. Improves patient outcomes by streamlining diagnostic processes and helping healthcare providers allocate resources more effectively.

Future Skills

Tools that help humans learn and adapt to new work. Personalized learning pathways that prepare individuals for the evolving job market and skill requirements.

Research Lab

Cutting-edge AI research focused on interpretability, robustness, and human-AI collaboration. We publish our findings and contribute to the open-source community.
