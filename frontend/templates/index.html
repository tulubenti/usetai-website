Our Mission

USETAI develops trustworthy AI and systems engineering to anticipate and solve complex human problems. We focus on building solutions that are interpretable, robust, and designed with human agency at the centre.

We partner with governments, NGOs, healthcare providers and industry to deploy responsible technology that measurably improves outcomes — from climate adaptation and public health to skills development and decision support.

Initiatives & Solutions

Climate-Assist

AI-driven scenario analysis and decision support for adaptation planning. We blend geospatial data, climate modelling and risk analytics to help organisations prepare for uncertainty.

Health Navigator

Workflow optimisation and triage tools for clinics and community health systems — improving throughput, triage accuracy and patient outcomes while respecting privacy and clinician oversight.

Future Skills

Personalised learning pathways and upskilling platforms that match individuals to evolving labour market needs and support lifelong career transitions.

Research Lab

Open research on interpretability, robustness and human-AI collaboration. We publish peer-reviewed work and share tools with the community.

Research & Publications

Our lab investigates safe, explainable, and resilient AI. We prioritise reproducible methods and open-source tooling to accelerate adoption of trustworthy practices.

  • Interpretability: methods that make model decisions transparent to domain experts.
  • Robustness: systems designed to degrade gracefully and allow human overrides.
  • Human-AI workflows: effective collaboration between people and models in high-stakes environments.
